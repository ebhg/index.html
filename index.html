What is Shabbat?

Shabbat is a sanctuary in timeâ€”a dedicated moment to disconnect from the busy week and reconnect with family, friends, and spirit.

The Three Pillars:

  • Rest (Menuchah): We pause from the endless cycle of creating and doing to find true physical and spiritual tranquility.
  • Distinctness/Holiness (Kedushah): We set this day apart from the ordinary, elevating the mundane into something sacred.
  • Joy (Oneg): We delight in the day through good food, relaxation, and the company of loved ones.
